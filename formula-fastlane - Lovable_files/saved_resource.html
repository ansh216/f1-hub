    F1 Rules (2025)

    • •Each team has 2 drivers.
    • •Budget cap: $135 million (2025).
    • •Sprint weekends included in selected races.
    • •New technical regulations for 2026, but 2025 continues with current aero rules.
    • •Tires: Pirelli sole supplier.
    • •Power units: Hybrid V6 turbo engines.
